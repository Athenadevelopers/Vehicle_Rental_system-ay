Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To rent a vehicle in Sri Lanka, you'll need a valid driver's license (international driving permit for non-Sri Lankan licenses), passport or national ID card, and a credit card for the security deposit. Additional documentation may be required for specific vehicle types.

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before pickup or no-shows are not eligible for a refund. Special terms may apply for long-term rentals.

Yes, we offer pickup and drop-off services at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) and Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport (HRI). Airport transfers can be arranged during the booking process for an additional fee. Our representative will meet you at the arrivals area with your vehicle.

Our standard rental price includes the vehicle rental, basic insurance coverage, 24/7 roadside assistance, and unlimited mileage for most vehicles. Additional services such as GPS navigation, child seats, additional drivers, and premium insurance coverage are available for an extra charge.

Yes, we offer chauffeur-driven services for all our vehicles. Our drivers are experienced, professional, and knowledgeable about Sri Lanka's roads and attractions. This option is popular for tourists who want to explore the country without the stress of driving in unfamiliar territory.
