Our Story

Founded in 2015, VehicleRent Pro Sri Lanka began with a simple mission: to provide tourists and locals with reliable, affordable, and convenient vehicle rental services across the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

What started as a small fleet of just 5 vehicles in Colombo has now grown to over 100 vehicles across multiple locations in Sri Lanka. Our journey has been one of continuous improvement, learning from our customers, and adapting to the unique needs of travelers in Sri Lanka.

Today, we're proud to be one of the leading vehicle rental services in the country, known for our exceptional customer service, well-maintained fleet, and commitment to making your journey through Sri Lanka as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
