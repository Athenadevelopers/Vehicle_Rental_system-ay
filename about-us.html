About VehicleRent Pro Sri Lanka

Your trusted partner for premium vehicle rentals across Sri Lanka

Our Story

Founded in 2015, VehicleRent Pro Sri Lanka began with a simple mission: to provide tourists and locals with reliable, affordable, and high-quality vehicle rental services across the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

What started as a small fleet of just 5 vehicles in Colombo has now grown to over 100 vehicles of various types, with pickup and drop-off locations in major cities and tourist destinations throughout Sri Lanka.

Our commitment to exceptional customer service, transparent pricing, and well-maintained vehicles has made us one of the most trusted vehicle rental companies in Sri Lanka, serving thousands of satisfied customers each year.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable vehicle rental services that enable our customers to explore Sri Lanka with confidence and freedom.

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our customers by maintaining our vehicles to the highest standards and providing 24/7 roadside assistance.

Customer Satisfaction

We go above and beyond to ensure our customers have a seamless and enjoyable rental experience from booking to return.

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact by maintaining fuel-efficient vehicles and implementing eco-friendly practices.
